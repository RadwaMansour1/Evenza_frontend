Frequently asked questions

Simply browse or search for the event you're interested in, select the number of tickets, and click “Book Now.” Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payment securely.

Refund policies vary by event organizer. Check the event’s page for specific refund terms. If eligible, refunds are usually processed within 7–10 business days.

You’ll receive a confirmation email with your ticket details. You can also log into your account to view all your bookings under the “My Bookings” section.

To create an account, find the 'Sign up' or 'Create account' button, fill out the registration form with your personal information, and click 'Create account' or 'Sign up.' Verify your email address if needed, and then log in to start using the platform.

Check your spam or junk folder. If it’s not there, log in to your account to check your bookings or contact our support team for assistance.
